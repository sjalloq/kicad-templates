Phoenix Contact

RPI-BC Expansion Board

This project template is the basis of an expansion board for the Phoenix Contact RPI-BC industrial housing for the Raspberry Pi.
This board is designed to mount on the RPi 40-pin GPIO header and provides external connections via four 4-pin PTSM feed-through headers.

This base project includes a PCB edge as specified by the DXF downloaded from the RPI-BC INT-PCB SET product page with the connectors placed correctly to align the two boards. All IO present on the Raspberry-Pi board is connected to the project through the 0.1" expansion headers. The 4-pin 2.5mm pitch PTSM feed-through connectors are also present.
